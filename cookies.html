Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how TRUEWEATHER HVAC uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, in accordance with the EU ePrivacy Directive, GDPR, and CCPA.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies — Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled. These include session management and security cookies.
  • Functional Cookies — Remember your preferences (e.g., language, region) to personalise your experience.
  • Analytics Cookies (with consent) — Help us understand how visitors interact with our site. Data is anonymised and aggregated. We use privacy-respecting analytics only.
  • Marketing Cookies (with consent) — Used to show you relevant content. We limit these to essential remarketing only.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our site, a consent banner will appear. You may accept all cookies, accept only essential cookies, or customise your preferences. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

How to Control Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Visit aboutcookies.org for browser-specific guidance. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

If we embed third-party content (e.g., Google Maps, YouTube), those providers may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy as technologies and regulations evolve. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
