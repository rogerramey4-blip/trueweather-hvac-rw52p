Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2026

TRUEWEATHER HVAC ("we", "us", or "our") operates this website and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and all applicable US state privacy laws effective in 2024–2026.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information — name, email, phone number, and address when you complete a contact form or request a quote.
  • Usage data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site (collected anonymously via server logs).
  • Cookie data — see our Cookie Policy for full details.
  • Communications — any messages you send us via email, phone, or web forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide quotes or estimates.
  • To fulfill service agreements and communicate project updates.
  • To send appointment reminders and follow-up communications (with your consent).
  • To improve our website and services using anonymised analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases: Contractual necessity (to provide services you request), Legitimate interests (to improve our services and communicate with prospects), Consent (for marketing communications — you may withdraw at any time), and Legal obligation (to comply with applicable law).

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (email platforms, scheduling tools, payment processors) who are contractually bound to protect it. We may disclose data if required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Opt-out of sale — California residents may opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell data).
  • Data portability — request your data in a portable format.
  • Restrict processing — request we limit how we use your data.

To exercise any right, contact us at trueweatherhvac@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain customer contact data for up to 3 years after last interaction, or as required by applicable law. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies required for the site to function and, with your consent, analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy for the full list and opt-out options.

8. Security

We implement HTTPS encryption, access controls, and industry-standard security measures to protect your data. No transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
